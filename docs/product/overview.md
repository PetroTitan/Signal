# Signal — product overview

Signal is an AI-assisted growth operations platform for founders and SaaS teams.

## What Signal optimizes for

Signal does not maximize posting volume. Signal maximizes sustainable organic presence. The goal is to make platform-native presence repeatable without the founder having to think about it every day, and without the platform-risk patterns that come from impulsive posting.

## What Signal helps founders do

- Maintain consistent platform-native presence.
- Avoid impulsive overposting.
- Reduce platform-risk behavior.
- Prepare a weekly growth plan.
- Approve once per week, in one calm review.
- Distribute activity organically across the week.

## What Signal is not

- Signal is not a spam bot.
- Signal is not an anti-detect browser.
- Signal is not an account farm manager.
- Signal is not a proxy or fingerprint system.
- Signal is not a mass automation tool.
- Signal is not a password manager.

## Initial target platforms

- Reddit
- X
- LinkedIn

## Operating loop

1. Signal assembles a weekly plan from product profiles, account states, and platform cadence.
2. The founder reviews the plan once, in the approval queue.
3. Approved items are distributed across the week with respect for cadence and cooldown.
4. The risk center flags anything that drifts from the product's allowed tone or platform's healthy cadence.
5. Analytics readiness exposes the schema that will report once WebmasterID is connected.
